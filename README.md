# Excel-Project Overview
<img width="1323" height="540" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3958c04e-05ea-4bad-be08-1e4aceea25e7" />

This is an end-to-end Excel Data Analysis project built on a simulated healthcare and pharmaceutical sales dataset. The project covers the full data analysis lifecycle — from raw data to an interactive dashboard with actionable business insights.
As a B.Pharma graduate with data analysis skills, this project bridges domain expertise with analytical thinking to generate real-world pharmaceutical business insights.

🎯 Objective
To analyze medicine sales, patient demographics, and regional profit data across India and provide meaningful business recommendations that can help pharmaceutical companies:
- Optimize inventory and stock planning
- Identify high-profit medicines and regions
- Understand patient demographics driving revenue
- Support marketing and distribution strategies


📁 Dataset Description
Column                              Description
Medicine ID                   Unique identifier for each medicine record
Medicine Name     Name of the medicine sold (e.g., Insulin, Atorvastatin)
Company Name      Pharmaceutical company supplying the medicine
Patient Name      Name of the patient
Age & Age Group   Patient age and category (Teenager / Adult / Senior)
Sex               Gender of the patient
Disease           Medical condition treated
Original Price    Cost price of the medicine
Total Cost        Selling price
Profit             Profit earned per transaction
% Profit          Profit margin percentage
Area / City / State / Country        Geographic location of the sale
Date              Date of transaction
Dataset Size: 100 records | Medicines: 15 unique types | Cities: 18+ cities across India

💡 Key Business Insights

💊 Top Medicines by Profit: Insulin and Atorvastatin generate the highest profit — primarily used for chronic diseases like Diabetes and Cholesterol. Recommendation: Increase stock and marketing investment for these medicines.
🏥 Top Diseases by Revenue: Skin Infection and Diabetes contribute the highest revenue and patient count across all cities.
📍 Top Cities: Bengaluru and Hyderabad are the top profit-generating cities. Recommendation: Expand distribution networks and focus sales efforts in these metropolitan areas.
👥 Patient Demographics: Adults and Seniors (40+ years) contribute the most revenue, mainly due to chronic disease treatment. Recommendation: Target marketing campaigns toward this age group.
🗺️ Top State: Maharashtra is the most profitable state overall, driven by high patient density in Pune and Mumbai.
